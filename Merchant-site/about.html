About

Who we are

Relationships and Integrity Matter

We bring unparalleled knowledge by uniting experts in transaction processing, security, and leading edge technology. The result, a secure payment platform with a simple, user-friendly interface that is integrated with the tools you need to manage payments and create operational efficiencies.

We know that serving our clients' best interest, serves our best interest. Our award winning team is here to provide you with the expert advice you need to survive and thrive. By surrounding ourselves with world-class partners who have proven best in class products we ensure our clients are poised for success.

A Customer-Centric Approach

In the changing world of financial payment processing you need more than a processor. You need a partner with the experience and knowledge to navigate increasing complexities. DigiPay Solutions delivers on all counts. We are proud to be known as nerds, gurus, and payment processing wonks.

No Setup Fees or Contracts

We want our clients to stay with us because we continually exceed their expectations, not because they are contractually obligated. Many gateway providers require lengthy contracts, and they can make it difficult to impossible for you to migrate your data. In some cases, once a third party provider tokenizes your data they claim ownership. We find this practice unacceptable. With DigiPay Solutions, you own your data and we refuse to hold clients hostage with strong-armed tactics. With no setup fees or contracts, and a team of exceptionally qualified integration experts, we make the transition and migration of your data simple and seamless.

What we Offer:

An award winning and highly experienced team

What sets us apart from the competition is our accomplished team. With decades of experience in merchant processing and technology, we eliminate points of pain with ROI-driven strategies that will improve process flow and profits.

Integrated payment Technology via a Cloud Based Hub

We architect and integrate enterprise-level payment solutions that keep up with the changing world of payment processing. Our cloud based HUB uses a single interface to access all the technology you need to create a secure, customized financial payment management system.
