Centralized Management

Flexible. Robust. Simple.

Albert Einstein famously said "Everything should be made as simple as possible, but not simpler"

This basic principle guides everything we do. We recognize that payment processing is unnecessarily complex, and merchants need a user-friendly enterprise-class solution that will adapt to our changing world. Our solution is elegant and simple.

One Interface. One Dashboard. Multiple Connections.

One Gateway Accommodates Multiple MID’s and Processors.

With DigiPay Solutions you can route transactions and manage multiple accounts in real time using a single sign-on. With clustered databases and cloud web servers our HUB is lightning-fast, scalable, and accepts 200+ currencies. In addition to credit card, ewallets, eCheck and ACH payments, merchants have access to both open and closed loop bank processing, mobile apps, loyalty and coupon management, advanced fraud prevention, tokenized payment data, and recurring billing.

Our advanced encryption algorithms were originally developed for the Department of Defense private label card processing. This assures you of the highest level of security. You can safely store client information in our ultra-secure vault.

Scalable. Customized. Unified.

We achieve this by architecting a cloud-based HUB with a host of plug-and-play modules to create the solutions you need today, and in the future. With best-in-class features already integrated into our gateway you save time and money. Need a more advanced solution? Ask. Our engineers are innovators and creators who aim to please.
