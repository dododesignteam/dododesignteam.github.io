Fraud and Chargeback Management

Preventing chargebacks and fraud is essential to maintain a healthy merchant account, and to avoid your account being frozen, or terminated. Using a single interface, we provide access to our global network of major card issuing banks and our award winning Chargeback Management Program.

We've got you covered

  • Avoid Chargebacks and Chargebacks Fees with Our Advance Notice Interceptor
  • Avoid Financial Loss by Stopping the Fulfillment of Fraudulent Orders in Process
  • Improve Your Fraud Prevention by Cancelling Orders Confirmed by the Cardholder As Fraud
  • Improve Cash Flow by Avoiding Reserve Accounts
  • Custom Fraud Rules and Filters Prevent Loss
  • Velocity Checking Prevents Hacking
  • Customer Scoring Blocks or Limits Problem Customers’ Spending
Chargeback Dispute

Monitoring and disputing chargebacks is essential to running a successful business. But chargebacks are not all the same and should not be treated that way. Our program experts will manage and dispute the chargeback process so you can reallocate staff and resources.

Revenue Recovery

MasterCard reports that recurring billing decline rates are averaging 25 to 30%. Are you losing customers and profits due to expired cards or transaction timing? Our proprietary algorithms update expiring cards and resubmit declined transaction to boost your bottom line.

Fraud Prevention

With proprietary FBI Tools and multiple layers of security we detect and avoid criminal intrusion and fraudulent transactions. Using our intelligent velocity checking merchants can set rules and limits to flag or block unusual transactions.
