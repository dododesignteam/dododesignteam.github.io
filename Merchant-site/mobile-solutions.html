Digital Wallet

Consumer adoption of mobile wallets is no longer a question of if, but when. Are you ready?

Merchants and consumers are looking for a mobile wallet that is more secure and convenient than plastic, and that can offer discounts and other incentives. Additionally, consumers want a digital wallet that will organize and store receipts, rewards, gift cards, and loyalty cards. They don’t want more apps. They want one wallet that will securely store multiple forms of payments, accrue rewards, and apply discounts at the time of purchase. Our integrated digital wallet delivers on all counts.

Consumer Benefits:
  • Ultra-Secure QR Technology Eliminates Storing Payment Data on Mobile Devices
  • Multiple Options for Check-Splitting and Tip
  • Credit Card Rewards Continue to Accrue, the Same as Plastic
  • Stores and Retrieves Receipts Quickly
  • Simplifies Expenses and Taxes by Adding Notes to Receipts
  • Stores and Organizes Discount Offerings
  • Consumer-Controlled Proximity Rewards
  • Identity Never Revealed
Merchant Benefits:
  • Ultra-Secure QR Technology Protects Consumers
  • Seamless, Frictionless Check-Out
  • QR Codes Increase Sales by Instantly Turning Screens, Paper, Kiosks, Even Jumbotrons into Sales
  • Compatible with All Major US Payment Networks
  • QR Technology Takes PCI Compliance Out of Scope
  • Unlike NFC Digital Wallets, No Equipment Needed
