Payment Gateway

Powerful Gateway. Single Interface.

Payment processing is increasingly complex and merchants need a gateway that creates enterprise-wide efficiencies. We provide a scalable and customized payment platform for global and domestic merchants.

The core of our system’s architecture is a cloud-based HUB with plug-and-play modules that are easy to implement and eliminate the need for time-consuming, costly integrations.

The Power of One:

One Gateway Accommodates Multiple MID’s and Processors.

With DigiPay Solutions you can route transactions and manage multiple accounts in real time using a single sign-on. With clustered databases and cloud web servers our HUB is lightning-fast, scalable, and accepts 200+ currencies. In addition to credit card, ewallets, eCheck and ACH payments, merchants have access to both open and closed loop bank processing, mobile apps, loyalty and coupon management, advanced fraud prevention, tokenized payment data, and recurring billing.

Our advanced encryption algorithms were originally developed for the Department of Defense private label card processing. This assures you of the highest level of security. You can safely store client information in our ultra-secure vault.

Scalable. Customized. Unified.

We support redirect integration and direct API integration. Our system has the unique capability of passing data to other systems engaged in the transaction. This not only creates a more secure environment, but also relieves merchants from managing the flow of data, and provides a clear boundary for PCI Compliance 2.0 (Level 1 Validated Payment Application).

We will customize rules with create thresholds to help you better manage your business and mitigate risk. With expert guidance every step of the way, we support you throughout the implementation, and beyond. Are you a high volume merchant?
