Why Integrated Merchant Services? Get The Advantage

As a business owner you have many choices. You can choose what products or services to sell, which location to set up shop and what price tag you want to charge your customers. And with every choice comes a unavoidable consequence.

If you choose a product no one wants, a location no one visits or ask a price people are unwilling to pay then you’ve set yourself up for failure. At Integrated Merchant Services we want to make sure that when you choose us to handle your credit card processing you know you’ve made the RIGHT choice.

At Integrated Merchant Services we have one main goal—to help you succeed. If your business grows and processes more, then we grow with you. With that in mind, we have built our systems to be customized to fit whatever stage of growth your business is in.

We will analyze your business from all angles and give you feedback and suggestions that can help you as you expand and grow.

We believe in keeping things simple:

  • NO Hidden Fees
  • NO Set up Cost
  • FREE Shipping
  • LOW Rates 
Grow Your Business

We are committed to you and your unique company and will make it our mission to help you prosper. We know that small businesses are not stamped out of a cookie cutter. Instead, each has its niche and caters to a unique set of customers with specific needs. Keeping your customers happy is the best way to guarantee that they will return to your time and time again.

Accept All Payments

We are offering you a FREE, comprehensive terminal solution so you can accept EMV cards, Apple Pay, Android Pay and all contactless forms of payment.

Accepting mobile payments is convenient for you and your customers. Integrated Merchant Services Pro turns a tablet into a robust fully functional POS terminal. Integrated Merchant Services Pro is tablet-based mobile POS system with all the features of traditional POS software.

Quickly and securely process customer transactions, accurately track sales and inventory, optimize employee labor and create detailed business reports—all from a mobile tablet device.

Through Integrated Merchant Services, you will have innovative credit card security technology (EMV), offering end-to-end encryption to protect your customer’s credit card data, making all card data completely useless to hackers and credit card thieves.
